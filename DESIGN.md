# Visual design system

This portfolio is dark-first.

- Use near-black and graphite backgrounds only; do not introduce white, cream, or light-gray page sections.
- Use white and cyan/teal tones for typography, icons, focus states, and emphasis. The primary accent is electric cyan (`#22D3EE`).
- Keep surfaces near-black with subtle cyan-teal borders and elevated cards slightly lighter than the page background.
- Avoid royal blue, green, red, warm beige, and light backgrounds unless an explicit error state requires it.
- New components must preserve accessible contrast: light text on dark surfaces, and dark text only inside intentionally bright blue action controls.
